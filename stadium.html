Old Trafford

Location: Sir Matt Busby Way, Old Trafford, Stretford, Manchester M16 0RA, UK

Old Trafford is a football stadium in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England, and the home of Manchester United. With a capacity of 74,140 seats, it is the largest club football stadium in the United Kingdom.
